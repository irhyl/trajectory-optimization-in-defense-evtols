Radar Equation (Threat Layer)

Detection probability under Swerling Case I (many equal-amplitude scatterers, scan-to-scan fluctuation):
P_d = P_fa ^ (1 / (1 + SNR))
SNR(R) = SNR_max * (R_max / R)^4
where SNR_max = log(P_fa) / log(0.9) - 1 ≈ 13.15 sets P_d=0.9 at R_max.
